CAPABLANCA
MEMORIAL FAIR 2022

Capablanca's Memorial remembers one of the greatest talented players of all time, in a joyful celebration filled with chess events like Simultaneous exhibitions, talks about the fascinating chess life of the great Cuban, his rivalries, best games, and much more. This year makes no exception and an array of speakers and activities assemble a great event.
